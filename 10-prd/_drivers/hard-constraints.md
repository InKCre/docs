# Hard Constraints

- platform or dependency limit: the product must preserve the separation between source capability, info-base authority, and sink usage.
- platform or dependency limit: extensions add capability through registration and lifecycle hooks rather than by bypassing core ownership boundaries.
- platform or dependency limit: product semantics must survive multi-runtime operation around the same info-base.
- staffing or maintainability limit: stable product language should stay small and shared, not duplicated per unit repo.
