# Business And Service Objectives

- business objective: provide one product that can collect information, organize it into a reusable base, and expose ways to use it later.
- service objective: keep source, info-base, and sink as distinct responsibilities even when one service implements multiple parts.
- service objective: allow extensions to add capability without forking core ownership boundaries.
- success rationale: reusable information should remain available across runtimes and downstream workflows instead of being trapped in collection-specific flows.
