# Scope

## In Scope

- automatic or event-driven collection of information from external systems
- organization of reusable information into blocks and relations inside the info-base
- retrieval, indexing, embedding, and downstream use of organized information
- extension-based capability addition for source, resolver, and sink behavior
- multi-runtime participation around the same shared info-base

## Out Of Scope

- implementation mechanism ordering
- module ownership topology and language-specific package structure
- wire transport internals and local interface details
- Spoke-local runtime operations and deployment procedures

## Open Question

- product pressure files are now present, but they should be expanded only when new product evidence or business decisions are explicitly recorded.
