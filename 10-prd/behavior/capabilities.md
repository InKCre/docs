# Capabilities

## Capability: Collection

- related claim(s): collect external information into reusable units
- non-goal: source configuration does not become the product's shared memory itself

## Capability: Info-base organization

- related claim(s): organize information in one reusable info-base
- non-goal: storage retrieval and resolver interpretation do not collapse into one generic blob reader

## Capability: Retrieval and downstream use

- related claim(s): expose organized information for retrieval and downstream use
- non-goal: sink usage does not take over source or info-base authority

## Capability: Extension-based capability growth

- related claim(s): collect external information into reusable units; expose organized information for retrieval and downstream use
- non-goal: extensions do not bypass core ownership boundaries

## Capability: Multi-runtime participation

- related claim(s): organize information in one reusable info-base
- non-goal: one runtime does not become the only valid participant in the shared system
